## 5. QUALITY STANDARDS & APPROVALS

### 5.1 Technical Standards

Audio must meet the following standards:
- **Format:** WAV (48kHz, 24-bit, mono)
- **Quality:** Broadcast-quality, no background noise, clicks, pops, or distortion
- **Levels:** Normalized to -16 LUFS (podcast standard)
- **Delivery:** No excessive breaths, clean edits

### 5.2 Test Recording

- GRANTOR will record 1 test chapter (paid)
- GRANTEE provides feedback within 3 business days
- GRANTOR adjusts delivery based on feedback
- Final approval required before full production

### 5.3 Revisions

**Included Revisions (No Additional Cost):**
- Pronunciation corrections
- Pacing adjustments
- Re-recording of chapters with technical issues (noise, distortion)

**Paid Revisions (Additional Cost):**
- Major script changes after initial recording
- Re-recording due to GRANTEE direction changes (not GRANTOR error)

### 5.4 Final Approval

- GRANTEE approves final delivery within 7 business days
- GRANTOR receives final edited files for review before public release
- Final payment triggered upon mutual approval
