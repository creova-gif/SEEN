## KEY FEATURES

### Narrator Casting Highlights

**Selection Philosophy:**
- Narrators are **witnesses**, not performers
- Voice direction: intimate, calm, reflective (not theatrical)
- Cultural competency required, heritage speakers preferred
- Long-form narration experience essential

**Casting Process:**
- 5 phases from audition to onboarding
- Blind initial review (no names, score on criteria)
- Committee consensus for final selection
- Cultural briefing before first session

**Compensation:**
- Industry-standard union rates ($300–500/hr)
- Work-for-hire agreements with platform usage rights
- Fair credit and recognition
- Long-term collaboration opportunity

---

### Expert Review Highlights

**Governance Principles:**
- **Advisory, not censorial** — improves quality, doesn't control creativity
- **Transparency** — all feedback documented with rationale
- **Right to appeal** — creators can challenge required changes
- **Harm prevention over perfection** — reviews prevent harm, not impose ideals

**Review Categories:**
1. **Cultural Advisors** — Ensure respectful representation
2. **Academic Reviewers** — Verify factual accuracy
3. **Accessibility Reviewers** — WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
4. **Language Reviewers** — Translation quality and localization

**Review Timeline:**
- Standard: 5–10 business days
- Urgent: 2–3 days (emergency only)
- Complex: 10–15 days (highly sensitive content)

## RISK MITIGATION

### Identified Risks & Solutions

**Risk:** Reviewer or narrator shortage  
**Solution:** Maintain 2× capacity roster, build org relationships, emergency recruitment budget

**Risk:** Timeline compression  
**Solution:** 2-week buffers in all schedules, expedited review process available, prioritize critical stories

**Risk:** Budget overruns  
**Solution:** Weekly cost tracking, approve all contracts before signing, 10% contingency fund

**Risk:** Cultural concern raised late (post-recording)  
**Solution:** Emergency advisory board convening, re-record budget allocated, escalation path defined

**Risk:** Quality issues in narration  
**Solution:** Clear QA criteria, retake provisions in contracts, alternate narrator on standby
