**Institution Level:** K-12 (High School), Undergraduate, Public Education, Museum/Archive  
**Curriculum Alignment:** Ontario Curriculum, Canadian History, African Canadian Studies, Migration Studies  
**Historical Scope:** 1783-1792 (Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Sierra Leone)

**Learning Objectives:**

- Evaluate systemic racism in colonial land distribution
- Examine primary sources (Book of Negroes, petition letters)

**Primary Sources Included:**
