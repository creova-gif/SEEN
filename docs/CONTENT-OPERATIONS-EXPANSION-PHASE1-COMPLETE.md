## PHASE 5: MUSIC CURATION LOGIC 🔄 IN PROGRESS

**Objective:** Define comprehensive music curation rules and expand catalog.

### Music Sources

- CREOVA Music (label content)
- Independent Black musicians
- Indigenous composers
- Asian diaspora artists
- Experimental cultural sound artists
- Community music archives

### Curation Principles

1. **Album/Experience-Based Listening**
   - NOT playlist chasing
   - Respect track order and artist intent
   - Full album experiences preferred

2. **Cultural Context Required**
   - Every music item must have cultural framing
   - Artist background and intent documented
   - Community connections explained

3. **No Algorithmic Virality**
   - NO engagement optimization
   - NO "you might also like" algorithms
   - Editorial curation only

4. **Discovery Rules**
   - Music appears in Explore (primary surface)
   - Selected pieces surface in For You (editorial picks)
   - Can be linked to Story Worlds or Collections
   - User-saved items appear in Library

### Music Item Structure

Each item includes:
- `musicId`
- Artist name
- Track / album title
- Cultural context (multilingual)
- Duration
- Playback source (hosted / embedded)
- Language notes
- Genre (non-commercial descriptors)
- Themes
- Linked content (stories, collections)
- Artist bio
- Cultural significance notes

**Status:** Framework defined. Catalog expansion in progress.

## PHASE 9: ANALYTICS (AGGREGATE ONLY) 🔄 IN PROGRESS

**Objective:** Define aggregate-only analytics that respect privacy.

### Metrics TO TRACK

**Story Engagement:**
- Story starts (total count, no individual IDs)
- Completion rates (percentage, aggregated)
- Average listen time
- Chapter progression (aggregated)

**Language Usage:**
- Language selection frequency (EN/FR/ES)
- Language switch patterns (aggregated)

**Audio Engagement:**
- Audio playback starts
- Audio completion rates
- Music listening time (aggregated)

**Community Participation:**
- Response submissions (count, no content stored without consent)
- Community feature usage (aggregated)

### Metrics TO NOT TRACK

❌ Individual behavior patterns  
❌ Emotional inference (sentiment analysis)  
❌ Cross-platform identity linking  
❌ Demographic profiling  
❌ Behavioral prediction  
❌ Engagement optimization metrics (for algorithmic ranking)  
❌ Location tracking (beyond country-level for compliance)  
❌ Device fingerprinting  
❌ Third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)

### CMF Reporting Templates

- Quarterly engagement reports
- Language usage summaries
- Content performance (for editorial decisions, not ranking)
- Accessibility feature usage
- Community impact metrics

### Opt-Out Enforcement Logic

Users can opt out of ALL analytics:
- Consent banner on first use
- Opt-out honored immediately
- No tracking after opt-out
- Periodic consent review (annual)
