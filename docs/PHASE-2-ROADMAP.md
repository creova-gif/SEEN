## DEPENDENCIES & RISK MITIGATION

### Critical Dependencies:

1. **Translation Services**
   - Risk: Vendor capacity/quality
   - Mitigation: Contract 2-3 vendors, built-in review cycles

2. **Voice Actors**
   - Risk: Talent availability/scheduling
   - Mitigation: Contract actors 3 months in advance, backup narrators

3. **Community Partnerships**
   - Risk: Partnership delays, feedback cycles
   - Mitigation: Early engagement, transparent timelines, paid consultation

4. **Studio Time**

   - Mitigation: Pre-book 6 months ahead, mobile studio option
