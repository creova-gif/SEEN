## COORDINATION CHECKPOINTS

### Checkpoint 1: Review Completion Before Recording

**Rule:** No narration recorded until expert review complete and revisions implemented.

**Rationale:**
- Prevents wasted recording costs
- Ensures narrator receives final, approved script
- Maintains cultural integrity

**Process:**
1. Story submitted for expert review
2. Feedback received and implemented
3. Final approval granted
4. Story marked "Ready for Recording"
5. Narrator receives finalized script

---

### Checkpoint 2: Narrator Availability Aligned with Review Timeline

**Rule:** Casting and review schedules coordinated to prevent bottlenecks.

**Strategy:**
- Cast narrators BEFORE reviews complete (they're independent)
- Schedule recording sessions AFTER review windows
- Build 2-week buffer between approval and recording

**Example Timeline:**

| Week | Content Review | Narrator Casting | Recording |
|------|----------------|------------------|-----------|
| 1–2 | Migration stories in review | EN auditions open | — |
| 3 | Review feedback received | FR auditions open | — |
| 4 | Revisions implemented | ES auditions open | — |
| 5 | Final approval | Narrators selected | — |
| 6 | — | Contracts finalized | Recording begins |

---

### Checkpoint 3: Quality Assurance Before Publication

**Rule:** All stories pass technical, cultural, and accessibility QA before going live.

**QA Layers:**
1. **Narration QA:** Audio quality, pacing, pronunciation
2. **Transcript QA:** Accuracy against audio (95%+ match)
3. **Cultural QA:** Reviewer spot-check of final product
4. **Accessibility QA:** WCAG compliance verification
5. **Technical QA:** Platform integration, playback, metadata

**Sign-Off Required:**
- ✅ Audio Producer
- ✅ Cultural Advisor (spot-check)
- ✅ Accessibility Lead
- ✅ Content Director

## CONTINGENCY PROTOCOLS

### If Review Delayed

**Impact:** Recording schedule shifts  
**Response:**
1. Notify narrators of delay
2. Reschedule recording sessions
3. Assess if other batches can proceed
4. Communicate timeline change to stakeholders

### If Narrator Unavailable

**Impact:** Recording cannot proceed as scheduled  
**Response:**
1. Activate alternate narrator (if contracted)
2. Re-cast from audition pool (expedited)
3. Adjust batch order (record available stories first)

### If Cultural Concern Raised Late

**Impact:** Story may need to be pulled or revised post-recording  
**Response:**
1. Emergency cultural advisory board meeting
2. Assess severity (minor revision vs. full re-record)
3. Implement changes or remove from publication
4. Document decision for CMF audit

## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Reviewer or Narrator Shortage

**Mitigation:**
- Maintain backup roster (2× capacity needed)
- Build relationships with cultural organizations
- Budget for emergency recruitment

### Risk 2: Timeline Compression

**Mitigation:**
- Build 2-week buffers into all schedules
- Prioritize critical stories (onboarding, featured)
- Have expedited review process available

### Risk 3: Budget Overruns

**Mitigation:**
- Track all costs weekly against budget
- Approve all contracts before signing
- Have contingency fund (10% of budget)
