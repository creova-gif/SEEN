## 🎬 Content Format Strategy

### Blog Posts
- **Long-form (2,000–3,000 words):** In-depth guides, research, founder stories
- **Medium-form (1,000–1,500 words):** How-tos, case studies, explainers
- **Short-form (500–700 words):** News, quick tips, product updates

### Video Content
**YouTube Series:**
- "Building in Africa" — monthly, 15–20 min
- "Product deep dives" — bi-weekly, 8–10 min (rotating through all 15 projects)
- Tutorials & how-tos — ongoing, 3–5 min
- Team interviews — monthly, 10–15 min
- "SEEN: Culture & Code" — fashion-meets-tech series, monthly

### Social Media
- **Twitter/X:** Daily insights, threads, engagement (3–5 posts)
- **Instagram:** Behind-the-scenes, visual storytelling (2–3 posts/day)
- **LinkedIn:** Professional insights, company updates (3x/week)
- **TikTok:** Product demos, team culture (2–3x/week per product)

### Email Newsletter
- **Substack:** Weekly insights (1,500 words)
- Topics: AI in emerging markets, founder journey, CREOVA portfolio updates, project milestones
